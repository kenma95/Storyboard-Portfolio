The evil organization the Hat, rising in a sudden. Lead by a young chairman Wit, they create chaos and spread fearness around the world. The FIB special agent, Nick, has to find his old friend, Kenley, a boy who has great intuition and bravery to end the chaos created by the Hat. Therefore, they step on the trip to the HQ of the Hat...

Creation Background

This is the narrative world created for the course Graphic Storytelling. Multiple projects were done to reveal the world. The main character, Kenley, is created in the first class assignment, however with unclear world settings. The basic storyline and settings of the world is basically buildup in thefourth assignment, which is the infographic design in non-linear order. More deepgoing world are constructed in my mind during the following Storyboards design.

About me

As a engineering student taking this heavily art course, I found it hard at the begining sketching, since I did not have systematically training in drawing. While the class going, I tried to switch the media from hand sketching to digital drawing, that really helped me a lot in the understanding of color blocks and with assist of computer, reduced the pressure of my lacking skills. Though challenging, I found the class interesting to show different world you created and tell the story in your own visual way. Although my drawing skills still need further study, I actually get great improvement of the techniques and storytelling skills.
