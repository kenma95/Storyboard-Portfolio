Character Design: Detailed vs Iconic

May 8, 2015 by Ruiqi Ma

The second part of character design is about to create different style of character. The left side graph is the detailed sketch of Kenley, with shadow and 3D prespective introudced. The right side is a highly stylish and abstract character design for Kenley.

Requirement for this assignment

Generate two images of your character.
Based on the content discussed in McCloud's Understanding Comics, create a heavily Detailed image of your character. References to 3-D space, form, and value should be evident.
Then, create an ICONIC version of your character. This version should be a more flat and minimal version.
