Project taH: to arrest Hat

Storyboard

May 7, 2015 by Ruiqi Ma

This is a single story create to reveal a new point of Kenley. Since no text can be used, I spend time on decribing the environment and detailed action of character to improve the readable of the comic. The story is just a few seconds actions, but that will take 16 pannel to make the story smooth. Also, this is my first time challenging drawing on detailed object, the train in this case.

Read the Synopsis...

Kenley is a person with special acute sense and always save friend‘s ass.
Kenley and Nick get into the train and sit together. Nick sit down with relieve, took out a cigarette, light it up and start smoking. Kenley felt discomfort because there is “No Smoking” sign in the cabin. Suddenly, he sensed that it is not only the smell of tobacco. It is the smell of dynamite! Someone put it inside Nick’s cigarette! Just in one moment, Kenley throw the cigarette, which is in the mouth of Nick out of the window, and laid themselves down. They heard the loud explosion outside of the window at the same time.

Requirement for this assignment

1. Generate a one sentence concept that will reveal something new about your character. Then, in a single paragraph, write a SHORT narrative that illustrates this concept.
2. Through a series of shots, show this story in pure visual form (no dialog or narration).
3. Each shot can contain either a single or multiple beats, depending on your use of storyboarding language.
