Project taH: to arrest Hat

Text = Text and Image Comic

May 7, 2015 by Ruiqi Ma

This is a story after the trip of Kenley and Nick on train. The story happens in the tunnel inside the Hat HQ, where Kenley met his ex- girlfriend. The text used in basically in the dialog form.

Read the Synopsis...

Kenley encountered his ex-girlfriend, now in the evil organization The Hat.
Kenley and Nick were walking in the secret tunnel to the base of the Hat. It was an ancient tunnel that there were only torches to light up.
“Jesus it is so dark here.” Said Nick. Suddenly wind blew and the torches were extinguished. Both of them had a bad feeling at the same time. “It’s a trap!” Nick lighted up his lighter. But it was too late. The tunnel started to light up, a girl held a pistol on Kenley. Nick responds quickly to point his pistol to the girl. Kenley slowly turn his head back to see the face of the girl and then astonished. “Emma! Why you are in the Hat?!”Said Kenley. “Shut up, you are not my boyfriend anymore. You two have only two choice, surrender or die.”
“Lay down your weapon!” Nick shouted to Emma.
Emma kept silent.
“Now!” Nick was getting nervous.
All three froze for a long while, Kenley finally gave up.
“Okay okay Emma, we surrender, it is your victory. Let’s give up, Nick.”

Requirement for this assignment

1. Generate a one sentence concept that will reveal something new about your character. Then, in a single paragraph, write a SHORT narrative that illustrates this concept.
2. Using any format of sequential stortelling, communicate this information in a combination of text (speech bubbles, narration) and image. Aim for a ratio of approximately 25% of you information communicated as text, 75% as image.

You will be assessed by:
How clearly you communicate your information
The craftsmanship of your visual execution
Your choices of what information to communicate visually, and what information you choose to communicate by text
