Requirement for this assignment

Building on examples of comics in which an entire page both works as a comic AND a graphic design, create your own sequential graphic design describing the environments of your imaged world. Characters must be a minimal element, or may not even be present. A focus should be on built environments, natural environments, or both. You sequence of images do not need to be linear, or in any way easy to "read".
