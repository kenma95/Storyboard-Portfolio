Project taH: to arrest Hat

Final Storyboard Design

May 9, 2015 by Ruiqi Ma

The final project of the class, I want to tell a completed short story happened in the world. Inspired by different aspect descrption used in my recommanded reading Barefoot Gen, I would like to change the vision from main character Kenley to Nick. Since they shall still have a long trip to the "final battle" and is difficult to end in 5 pages, so the story plot for the prequel story of the Hat rising, is generated.

Read the Synopsis...

It is in the house of the city. The wife are cooking the breakfast.
“Sorry daring, I have to go.” Nick said. “You have important works to do today? “Asked by his wife. “You know, the world is getting more and more dangerous these days.”
“Take care of yourself” Wife hugged Nick. Nick drank the coffee. He opened the door and leaves his room. Nick walks in a building. A fully armed security guard asked him for ID card.
My name is Nick. I didn’t tell my wife I am the agent of FIB. The world is changing greatly these days. War burst in many third world nations. Some leaders were assassinated, while leaving a strange symbols around. Meeting Room, the people wearing black suites sitting in front of the round table.
The man sit in front of the table. “So, what did you want to report about the anarchy in these months, Richard?”
Richard, a mid-age man has his floor. “Yes, I found out the mastermind of these cases. It’s the Hat.” The Hat is an evil organization that suddenly arose this year. They have a number of elite in every field and have the strong financial support. It is not likely an organization that is supported by any nations recognized, but all by a single young man.
“Oh, that sounds interesting. But is that our business?”
“What are you saying, Chairman! The evil organization will contaminate our land soon if we don’t take action! Are we just sit here to wait for the crisis?”
On the other hand, bell rings in Nick’s home. Nick’s wife thinks that it’s Nick that left something at home. However, the person out there is not Nick, it seems like a hitman.
Come back to the meeting, suddenly, blood burst from Richard’s chest. It is the chairman who is holding the handgun. “I know there is traitor among us, but I cannot believe you are the black sheep…”
The attenders of the meeting stand and head their guns to the chairman.
“You are still too naïve.” The chairman smiles. Sudden explosion happens in the meeting room. People blow out everywhere.
Nick hardly stand up from the dust. His cellphone rings. It is his wife. “Go find Kenley.” He can hear his wife in a voice of nervous.
“Don’t move! What are you doing?!” A man’s shouting also in the cell phone. Nick quickly know she is in risk but had no idea, he had to find Kenley, his old friend.
